Paneer Tikka

Description

Paneer Tikka is a popular Indian appetizer made from marinated paneer cubes grilled with spices and vegetables. It's known for its smoky flavor and vibrant taste, often served with mint chutney.

Ingredients

  • Paneer (Indian cottage cheese)(cubed)
  • Thick curd
  • Ginger paste
  • Red chilli powder

Steps

  1. Prepare the marinade: In a bowl, mix cup thick yogurt, 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste, 1 teaspoon red chili powder, teaspoon turmeric powder, teaspoon garam masala, teaspoon chaat masala, 1 teaspoon coriander powder, 1 tablespoon lemon juice, 1 tablespoon mustard oil, and salt to taste.
  2. Marinate the paneer: Add 200 to 250 grams of paneer cubes to the marinade. Mix gently to coat all pieces. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
