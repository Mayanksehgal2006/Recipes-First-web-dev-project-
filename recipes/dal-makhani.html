Dal Makhani

Description

Dal Makhani is a rich and creamy North Indian dish made from black lentils and kidney beans slow-cooked with butter and cream. It has a deep, smoky flavor and is traditionally enjoyed with naan or rice.

Ingredients

  • Whole black urad dal
  • Rajma
  • Ginger
  • Green chilli

Steps

  1. Soak the dals: Rinse 1 cup whole black urad dal and cup rajma (kidney beans) thoroughly. Soak them together in enough water for 6-8 hours or overnight.
  2. Cook the dals: Drain the soaked dals and pressure cook them with 4 cups water until soft (usually 5-6 whistles).
